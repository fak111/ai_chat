# A宝 Logo 设计提示词

## 产品理念提炼

| 关键词 | 含义 |
|--------|------|
| A = AI | 智能、科技感 |
| 宝 = 宝贝/珍宝 | 温暖、亲切、可爱 |
| 群聊 | 社交连接、多人互动 |
| 熟人网络 | 信任、亲密、归属感 |
| AI 融入社交 | 不是冷冰冰的工具，而是群里的"伙伴" |

**核心调性**: 温暖科技感 — 既有 AI 的智慧感，又有熟人社交的亲切感

---

## 提示词方案

### 方案一：气泡伙伴（推荐）

> A minimalist mobile app logo for "A宝", an AI group chat application. The design features a stylized speech bubble cluster — three overlapping rounded chat bubbles in soft gradient colors (warm coral, sky blue, and light violet), symbolizing group conversation. Inside the central bubble, a friendly abstract "A" letterform with subtle circuit-line accents hints at AI intelligence. The bubbles are slightly tilted and playfully arranged to convey warmth and social energy. Clean white background, flat design with soft shadows, modern and approachable. No text in the logo mark itself.

### 方案二：星座连接

> A modern app icon for "A宝", an AI-powered social chat app. The logo depicts 4-5 small glowing dots (representing friends) connected by thin luminous lines forming a constellation-like network, with one slightly larger dot in the center radiating a gentle warm glow — representing the AI companion among friends. Color palette: deep indigo to soft purple gradient background with warm gold and white connection lines. Geometric yet organic feel, conveying both technology and human warmth. Minimal, icon-friendly, works at small sizes.

### 方案三：微笑字母

> A playful yet sophisticated app logo featuring the letter "A" reimagined as a cute character. The crossbar of the "A" forms a gentle smile, and two small dots above the crossbar serve as eyes, creating a friendly face. The letter has rounded, bubbly proportions with a soft gradient from warm peach to light magenta. A tiny speech bubble floats near the top-right corner with a sparkle inside, suggesting AI magic. Flat design, no outlines, suitable for app icon. Background: clean white or very light warm gray.

### 方案四：拥抱圆环

> A warm, modern logo for a social AI app called "A宝". The design shows an abstract group hug — three to four simplified human silhouettes arranged in a circle, arms subtly connecting, with a glowing AI spark (small starburst) nestled in the center of their embrace. Soft rounded shapes, pastel color palette (mint green, soft orange, lavender, pale blue). Conveys inclusivity, friendship, and intelligent companionship. Flat vector style, works perfectly as a circular app icon.

## 配色建议

| 方案 | 主色 | 辅色 | 情感 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 气泡伙伴 | `#FF6B6B` 珊瑚红 | `#4ECDC4` 薄荷绿 / `#A78BFA` 淡紫 | 活力、友好 |
| 星座连接 | `#312E81` 深靛蓝 | `#FCD34D` 暖金 / `#E0E7FF` 浅蓝紫 | 深邃、信赖 |
| 微笑字母 | `#F472B6` 桃粉 | `#FBBF24` 明黄 / `#FFF1F2` 浅粉白 | 可爱、灵动 |
| 拥抱圆环 | `#34D399` 薄荷绿 | `#FB923C` 暖橙 / `#C4B5FD` 淡紫 | 温馨、包容 |

## 使用建议

- 以上提示词适用于 Midjourney / DALL-E / Stable Diffusion 等 AI 图像生成工具
- 生成后建议用矢量工具（Figma/Illustrator）精修为标准 SVG
- App Icon 尺寸：1024x1024px（iOS/Android 通用母版）
- 建议同时生成圆角方形（iOS）和圆形（Android）两个版本
